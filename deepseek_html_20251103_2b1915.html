Welcome to the Trinbarjam Elections Commission

The Trinbarjam Elections Commission is responsible for organizing and overseeing all national and local elections in Trinbarjam. Our mission is to ensure free, fair, and transparent elections that reflect the will of the people.

This website provides comprehensive information about the recent general elections, including results by constituency, candidate information, and party performance.

Recent Election Overview

The 2026 General Elections saw a high voter turnout across all eight constituencies. Four political parties contested the elections:

  • Democratic Action Party (DAP)
  • Workers National Alliance (WNA)
  • United Progressive Movement (UPM)

Political Parties

Four political parties contested the 2026 General Elections in Trinbarjam. Below is information about each party.

Democratic Action Party (DAP)

Leader: Bruno Fernandes

Founded: 1998

Ideology: Centrism, Social Liberalism

Total Votes (2026): 12,389

Seats Won: 1

The Democratic Action Party advocates for balanced economic growth, social justice, and environmental sustainability.

Workers National Alliance (WNA)

Leader: Emma Myers

Founded: 2005

Ideology: Social Democracy, Labor Rights

Total Votes (2026): 19,319

Seats Won: 3

The Workers National Alliance focuses on workers' rights, public services, and economic equality.

United Progressive Movement (UPM)

Leader: Mohammed Salah

Founded: 2010

Ideology: Progressivism, Reformism

Total Votes (2026): 14,479

Seats Won: 3

The United Progressive Movement advocates for political reform, technological advancement, and social progress.

People's Democratic Revolution (PDR)

Leader: Jude Bellingham

Founded: 2015

Ideology: Democratic Socialism, Anti-Corruption

Total Votes (2026): 8,423

Seats Won: 1

The People's Democratic Revolution focuses on wealth redistribution, anti-corruption measures, and grassroots democracy.
